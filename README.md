# Career OS

Career navigation for Asia. Treats a career as one continuous thing - not a series of disconnected job applications.

Built for [Tech Hackathon 2026](https://www.techhackathon.com/starter-kit).

---

## The problem

LinkedIn, JobStreet, and most ATS tools see snapshots - a job posting, a resume filter, a graduation date. Nobody sees the full arc: the plateaus, the pivots, the gaps for health or family, the way skills compound or decay over 40 years. Career OS is built around that longer view.

## Core design decisions

- No black-box scores. When the system makes a recommendation, it explains the reasoning and shows where the uncertainty is.
- Career gaps are context, not penalties. A two-year break for caregiving or study is first-class data.
- Bidirectional from day one. The same data layer serves candidates and employers, not two separate products bolted together.
